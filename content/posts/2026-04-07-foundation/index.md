Twenty-two years of writing scattered across WordPress backups, loose HTML files, and Google Docs. Today I built the home that pulls them all together. Hugo, a custom theme, Cloudflare Pages. The whole thing is managed by Claude Code, which means the commit history is half mine and half a robot's. Suits me.

The plan is simple. Get the foundation working. Then import the food posts. Then adventure. Then dance. Tech is the new bit, where I'll write about how the AI is doing the work.

If you're reading this on day one, the rest of the site is empty. Come back in a few weeks.
