# Social posts - Hoi aka Hoiboy

Paste-ready social copy for this feature. This file does not publish (it is a Hugo
bundle resource, not a page); it is the cheat-sheet for posting.

Post native. Social platforms downrank posts with a link in the body, and for the
community, shares matter more than clicks to hoiboy.uk (that traffic comes on its
own). So the Source link goes in the first comment, never in the post body.

Feature link (the Source, used in the first comment on every post):

```text

```

## Post 1 - Full story (Facebook post, or LinkedIn Article)

Post the full feature story natively. The LinkedIn feed hard-caps at ~3,000
characters, so on LinkedIn publish it as an Article, not a feed post. Facebook has
room for the whole thing. Then append these hashtags.
