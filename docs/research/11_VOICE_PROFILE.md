## Voice cutoff rule (load-bearing)

Posts split into two universes by date.

- **Date < 2026-04-07** = legacy corpus. **Voice-sacred. NEVER edit the prose.** These are evidence of pre-AI Hoi's voice and the persona research itself. Cleanup is structural only (encoding, dead links, image rehost). See `02_BLOG_IMPORT_PIPELINE.md` for the import contract.
- **Date >= 2026-04-07** = new posts. Voice rules below apply. Run `check-ai-writing-tells.py` (see `../../../dotfiles/SST3/scripts/`) before publishing. RAG from this file plus the canonical `VOICE_PROFILE.md` in dotfiles before drafting anything.

The foundation post `2026-04-07` is the boundary marker. Anything dated on or after that day is "new" Hoi-voice content where AI contamination matters.

## The single biggest mistake

Going TOO smooth, TOO polite, TOO AI. Hoi's published voice is ~95% identical to his private draft voice. Don't sterilise him. He keeps profanity, exclamation marks, parenthetical asides, self-deprecation, and intensifier stacking ("very very very") in his public writing. Match that.

## The five things that will make a post sound fake

1. **The em-dash character (U+2014).** ZERO tolerance. Verified across 37 blogs: Hoi has zero. The single loudest AI signature. Use commas, colons, parentheses, ellipses, or two sentences.
2. **Smooth uniform sentence length** averaging 25-35 words. Hoi swings 4 -> 50 -> 8. Lumpy, not smooth.
3. **No exclamation marks.** Hoi uses 30-100 per long blog in high-energy content. Use them.
4. **No parenthetical asides.** Hoi drops them constantly (this is where his personality lives).
5. **Rule-of-three abstract nouns** ("presence, curiosity, and shared experience"). Replace with concrete rule-of-8 lists.

## Sentence rhythm fingerprint

- **Average:** ~16 words. **Median:** ~13 words.
- **Range:** 1 word ("Gone." / "Yum." / "WRONG.") up to ~58 words.
- **Distribution:** Lumpy right-tailed. Mode in the 8-14 word band, frequent 30-50 word run-ons, frequent 1-4 word snaps.
- **Default pattern:** medium-medium-LONG-short. Build context, expand with comma-stacked clause, land on a punch.
- **Three-beat structure (Claim -> Subvert -> Pivot)** is his most reliable pacing mechanism. Always claim, twist, deeper point.

**Verbatim examples (use these as templates):**

I wasn't good with meeting strangers, I wouldn't even know how to start a conversation or what to do with awkward silences. At the age of 21, I would still get my best friend to help call up ordering takeaways because I was scared to talk to strangers!" (#01)
>
> "Was I shaking? Yes. Was I scared? Not until I looked at down. Am I crazy? ...Maybe." (#13)
>
> "Gone. Kaput!" (#01)
>
> "Enough was enough. The decision to take ownership began with built-up frustration and the final thoughts of 'fuck it, I'm doing it'." (#01)

## Sentence opening distribution (sampled across 5 blogs)

- "I" / "I've" / "I'm", 22% (highest)
- "You" / "You're", 11%
- "The / This / That / These" combined, 14%
- Conjunction openers ("And", "But", "So", "Now", "However"), 12%
- Imperatives ("Stop", "Try", "Don't", "Remember"), 8%
- Questions, 6%

**Never opens with:** Furthermore, Moreover, Additionally, In today's world, In the realm of.

## Punctuation profile

| Mark | Use |
|---|---|
| Em dash (U+2014) | NEVER. Single loudest AI tell. |
| En dash `–` | Rare, OK as bullet separator. |
| Hyphen `-` | Conversational, fine. |
| Ellipsis `...` / `....` | HEAVY. Every 2-4 paragraphs. Trailing thoughts, pauses, comic timing. 3 dots normal, 4+ for extra effect. |
| Exclamation `!` / `!?` | 30-100 per long blog in high-energy content. Use generously. |
| Parentheses `(...)` | VERY HEAVY. Personality lives here. Use generously. |
| Question `?` | Heavy, stacked 2-3 in a row. |
| Semicolon `;` | Occasional. |
| Italics `*word*` | Emphasis and quoted dialogue. |
| Bold | Numbered list headings and key phrases. Not every bullet. |
| Emoji | Zero in long-form. Medium in old ZoukBase (`:)`, `:P`, `:D`). HIGH in FB-era posts. |

## Verbal tics

- **Spelled-out caps** for emphasis: `S H A R E`, `WIN:WIN:WIN`, `STOP CHASING WOMEN`, `BIG NEWS`.
- **All-caps single-word emphasis**: WRONG. STOP. MANY. Sparingly, 1-2 per blog max.
- **Stretched spelling when excited**: "wineeeeeeee", "Nooo", "Omg", "aaaaalllll".
- **Repetition as incantation**: "Stop chasing women… Stop chasing women…" (7x).
- **Parenthetical asides everywhere**: "(MANY)", "(I've seen my videos)", "(without bragging too much)", "(hehe)".
- **Rhetorical question stacks**: 2-3 questions in a row. "?!" combo when frustrated.
- **Single-word verdict fragments**: "Yum.", "Worth it.", "Too cool.", "Kaput!"

## Confidence markers (asserting without bragging)

1. **Specific numbers do the boasting.** Not "saved a lot", "$599 -> $285, saving $269,263/year". The number IS the brag.
2. **Contrast framing ("Most people X, I Y").** His most reliable rhetorical move. Examples:
   - "Most people use AI as autocomplete. I treat it like a junior-to-mid engineer that I'm tech-leading."
   - "My old approach was X. My new approach is Y. Can you see the difference?"
3. **Self-deprecation as a flex**: "being the very untalented dancer that I am", "I have a memory of a goldfish and know only 8 moves", "short-arse at only 5ft 7 inches".

## Practical workflow for writing a new post

1. **RAG first**. Read this file PLUS the full canonical at `../../../dotfiles/cv-linkedin/VOICE_PROFILE.md` (Section 12 has verbatim sentence templates). Generic = lazy.
2. **Draft loose**. Don't filter. Get the story out.
3. **Voice pass**:
   - Search and destroy every em-dash character (U+2014)
   - Search and destroy every banned word from the anti-vocab list
   - Read aloud, every stumble point is AI phrasing, rewrite it
   - Check sentence rhythm, break up any long string of medium-length sentences with a 1-4 word snap
   - Add at least 2 parenthetical asides (personality)
   - Add intensifier stacking somewhere ("really really")
4. **Run** `python3 ../../../dotfiles/SST3/scripts/check-ai-writing-tells.py content/posts/<slug>/index.md` before commit.
5. **CI sanity**: em-dash CI guard does NOT cover `content/posts/` (legacy is exempt). For new posts, the AI-tells script is your local gate. Don't rely on CI.
6. **Test**: "Could any other blogger have written this?" If yes, you skipped retrieval. Start over.
