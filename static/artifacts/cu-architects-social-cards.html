CU Architects: automated social share cards

A HOIBOY AI LTD build: every page generates its own 1200x630 share card

hoiboy.uk

Every page of the CU Architects website generates its own 1200×630 social share card, so a shared link shows a proper preview instead of a generic logo. Pages with their own image use it; everything else gets an on-brand card built from the practice's own logo, fonts and palette. It all happens automatically as part of the managed build. Below is every card the live site is serving today.

Pages with a hero use their own image

Project pages and service pages each have a strong hero, cropped to the social size at build time. Publish one, and its card appears with no extra step.

Everything else gets a branded card

Pages with no photo (home, regions, councils, guides, the section indexes) get an on-brand card generated from a one-line manifest, in the practice's own identity.

One source, always on brand

The layout, colours and the wordmark come straight from the brand guidelines, so every card is consistent and correct. Change the copy in one place and regenerate.
