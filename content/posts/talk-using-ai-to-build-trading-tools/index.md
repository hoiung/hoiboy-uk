On Saturday the 13th June 2026, I gave a talk to the London Investments and Traders Group. The topic was "Using AI to build trading tools".

It turned out fruitful, where I originally created this talk to be only about 30 mins long, and left 30 mins for Q&A. It went on for 2+ hours with many questions and discussions and debates. This for me was a very positive feedback because so many people were interested and very engaged, hence why they managed to hang around for that long lol.

There was one topic that slipped my mind since my slide deck went back and forth quite a few times, and that was [Why Does AI Hallucination Keep Happening?](/posts/why-ai-hallucination-keeps-happening/) It's fine, I followed up with the group and also finished my more comprehensive blog on this topic if you click on the hyperlink.

I built this presentation for entry level AI users in mind, people who are less technical in IT but are still subject matter experts or domain experts of their own accord. But also useful for those who are starting out in their professional life and still learning. Basically covering the grounds for users who probably used it as far as the web AI chat prompts.

I'm making my presentation slider available here with my keynotes. It's a very lite introduction and short bullet points of my keynotes (not the details since that was my vocal talk since I try to avoid scripted talks and try and feel the crowd and their needs filling in the gaps as necessary with my expertise and knowledge in Tech & AI and more). Most of my knowledge if you missed my talk can be found on this blogsite, and obviously I may not put all my knowledge into words yet if you find gaps, so more blogs to come as I have time to sit and write.
